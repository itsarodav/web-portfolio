If you’ve peeked around here, thank you! Let’s switch into chat mode :). It all started with music. Before I even knew what I wanted to do, I was obsessed with rhythm, layers, tiny details, and the music industry itself. That curiosity led me to graphic design: logos for bands, cover art, and identities for creative projects (and a few social ones, too). I’ve also always been a computer kid, so my interest in code and how websites and apps are built has been there from day one.

These days I work as a design engineer after a path through product design. I care about the connection between technology and people: how we use apps every day, how they integrate with devices, and what makes an experience feel smooth, accessible, simple, and consistent. Music runs in parallel as Marces (Spotify link for now, website coming soon), and I’m serious about growing that project as an independent artist.

Outside of work, I’m big on ergonomics and optimizing setups (yes, I’ll look at your desk and immediately diagnose it). I also disappear into device and app reviews, especially where tech meets music. I read a lot, keep doing branding for fun, and sometimes invent projects just to give them an identity and bring them to life in code. I share that exploration through lemonims, my personal lab and small creative label.
